# Muqaddimah

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 1377 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** North Africa (Ibn Khaldun)
**Original language:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Sociology, historiography, statecraft

## Summary

Ibn Khaldun's 'Muqaddimah' — a prolegomenon to history proposing a science of society, climate, and the rise/fall of dynasties.

## Why it matters

Often called the first work of sociology and economics; a mirror-for-princes of civilizational cycles.
