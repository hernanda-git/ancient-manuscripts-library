# Kitab Al Filaha

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 12th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Al-Andalus (Ibn al-Awwam)
**Original language:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Agriculture, agronomy

## Summary

Ibn al-Awwam's 'Book of Agriculture' — a comprehensive Andalusi agronomy manual covering crops, soils, irrigation, and pests.

## Why it matters

The most important medieval Arabic agricultural treatise; transmitted Andalusi and Near Eastern farming knowledge.
