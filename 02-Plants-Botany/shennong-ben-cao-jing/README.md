# Shénnóng Běn Cǎo Jīng

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 1st–2nd c. CE (Han dynasty)
**Origin / Tradition:** China
**Language of text:** Chinese (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Pharmacology, herbal medicine

## Summary

The 'Divine Farmer's Classic of Materia Medica' — the earliest Chinese pharmacopoeia, cataloguing 365 substances by their medicinal properties.

## Why it matters

The foundation of Chinese materia medica; the Chinese counterpart to Dioscorides.
