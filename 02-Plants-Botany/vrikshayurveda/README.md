# Vrikshayurveda

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 10th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** India
**Language of text:** Sanskrit (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Arboriculture, plant care

## Summary

Sanskrit treatises (notably by Surapala) on the science of plant life and tree cultivation — an 'ayurveda of trees'.

## Why it matters

A rare dedicated South Asian work on arboriculture and plant physiology.
