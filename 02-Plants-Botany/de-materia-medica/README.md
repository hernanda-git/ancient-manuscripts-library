# De Materia Medica

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 50–70 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Greek (Anazarbus, Roman Syria)
**Language of text:** Greek (English translation)
**Modern discipline(s):** Pharmacology, botany, materia medica

## Summary

Dioscorides' five-book pharmacopoeia describing ~600 medicinal plants and their uses — the standard medical botany for 1500 years.

## Why it matters

The authoritative herbal of Europe and the Islamic world until the Renaissance; basis of the illustrated herbal tradition.
