# Canon Of Medicine

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 1025 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Persia (Ibn Sina / Avicenna)
**Original language:** Arabic/Persian (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Medicine, pharmacology, botany

## Summary

Avicenna's 'al-Qanun fi al-Tibb' — the canon of Greco-Islamic medicine, with extensive botanicopharmacological sections.

## Why it matters

The standard medical textbook of both Islamic and European medicine for six centuries.
