## Summary

The 'Compendium of Materia Medica' — Li Shizhen's monumental 16th-c. Chinese pharmacopoeia cataloguing ~1900 substances.

## Why it matters

The culminating work of classical Chinese pharmacology; among the most influential natural-history books ever written.
