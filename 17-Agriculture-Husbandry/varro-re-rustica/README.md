# De Re Rustica (Rerum Rusticarum Libri Tres)

**Author / Tradition:** Marcus Terentius Varro

**Era / Date:** 37 BCE

**Origin / Tradition:** Rome

**Language:** Latin (original)

**Related manuscripts in this library:** Columella's *De Re Rustica* (verbatim, la); Cato's *De Agri Cultura* (pointer); Geoponica (pointer)

## Summary

Varro's three books on agriculture — covering estate management, livestock, and crops — the most systematic Republican-Roman agricultural treatise, now partially lost.

## Why it matters

A key source for Roman farming, alongside Cato and Columella, preserving practical agronomy and Republican rural economy.
