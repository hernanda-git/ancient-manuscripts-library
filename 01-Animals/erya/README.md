# Erya

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 3rd c. BCE
**Origin / Tradition:** China (Zhou/Han)
**Language of text:** Chinese (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Lexicography, early natural history

## Summary

The oldest surviving Chinese dictionary, organized by semantic categories including chapters on plants, trees, birds, beasts, fish, and insects.

## Why it matters

A foundational Chinese lexicographic and proto-encyclopaedic text; its categorical arrangement prefigures later natural-history classification.
