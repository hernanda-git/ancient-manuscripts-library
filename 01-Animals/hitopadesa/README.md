# Hitopadeśa

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 12th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** India
**Language of text:** Sanskrit (English translation)
**Modern discipline(s):** Fables, political wisdom, animal tales

## Summary

A Sanskrit collection of fables built from the Panchatantra, framed as instruction in statecraft and practical wisdom through animal stories.

## Why it matters

A major vehicle for the spread of Indian fable literature into Persian, Arabic, and European traditions.
