# Kitab Al Baytarah

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 9th–12th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Islamic world (veterinary tradition)
**Original language:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Veterinary medicine, hippology

## Summary

The Arabic equine/veterinary tradition (e.g. Ibn al-Akfani, al-Malik al-Mujahid's al-Baytara) on horse care, disease, and husbandry.

## Why it matters

Represents the advanced Islamic veterinary science that fed into European hippiatry.
