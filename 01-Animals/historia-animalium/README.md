# Historia Animalium (History of Animals)

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 350–343 BCE
**Origin / Tradition:** Ancient Greece (Lyceum)
**Language of text:** Greek (English translation)
**Modern discipline(s):** Zoology, natural history, biology

## Summary

Aristotle's nine-book inquiry into the structure, generation, behaviour, and classification of animals — the first systematic zoology in the West.

## Why it matters

The bedrock of biological science for nearly two millennia; established observation-based classification that Linnaeus later extended.
