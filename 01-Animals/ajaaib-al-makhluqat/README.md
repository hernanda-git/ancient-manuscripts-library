# Ajaaib Al Makhluqat

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 1260 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Persia/Iran (al-Qazwini)
**Original language:** Arabic/Persian (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Cosmography, wonders of creation, bestiary

## Summary

Zakariyya al-Qazwini's 'Aja'ib al-Makhluqat wa Ghara'ib al-Mawjudat ('Wonders of Creation') — a cosmography spanning cosmos, geography, plants, animals (including jinn and monsters), and humankind.

## Why it matters

The most copied illustrated cosmography of the Islamic world; a key bridge between classical science and the 'marvellous'.
