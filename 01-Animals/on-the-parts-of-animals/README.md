# On the Parts of Animals

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 350 BCE
**Origin / Tradition:** Ancient Greece
**Language of text:** Greek (English translation)
**Modern discipline(s):** Comparative anatomy, zoology

## Summary

Aristotle's treatise on the parts (organs and structures) of animals and their functional purpose, grouped by homology.

## Why it matters

Pioneered comparative anatomy and the teleological study of form; companion to the History of Animals.
