# Naturalis Historia (Natural History)

**Author:** various

**Origin / Tradition:** Rome (Pliny the Elder)

**Modern discipline(s):** Encyclopaedics, zoology, botany, mineralogy

## Summary

Pliny's 37-book Natural History — a vast encyclopaedia of the known world, with major books on land/marine animals, botany, and minerals.

## Why it matters

The single most copied scientific work of antiquity; the default reference encyclopedia of the medieval West.
