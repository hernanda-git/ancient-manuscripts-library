# One Thousand and One Nights

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** 8th–14th c. CE (formed)
**Origin / Tradition:** Middle East / South Asia / Persia
**Language of text:** Arabic (English translation)
**Modern discipline(s):** Folklore, frame narrative, marvellous tales

## Summary

The frame-tale of Scheherazade — a vast cycle of folk tales, jinn, voyages, and wonders spanning the Islamic world.

## Why it matters

The defining work of Arabic narrative fiction and a core source for the 'marvellous' and for jinn lore (see 05-Jinn-Unseen).
