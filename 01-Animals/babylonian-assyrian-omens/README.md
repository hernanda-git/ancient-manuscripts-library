# Babylonian Assyrian Omens

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 2000–500 BCE
**Origin / Tradition:** Mesopotamia
**Original language:** Akkadian (original, cuneiform)
**Modern discipline(s):** Divination, zoological omens

## Summary

The Mesopotamian omen series (e.g. Summa izbu, Summa alu) that read animal births, behaviour, and appearances as signs from the gods.

## Why it matters

The largest body of cuneiform divinatory literature; shows how animals were woven into cosmic signalling.
