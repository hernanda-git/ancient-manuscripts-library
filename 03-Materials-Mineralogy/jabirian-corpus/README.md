# Jabirian Corpus

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 8th–9th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Iraq (Jabir ibn Hayyan / Geber)
**Original language:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Alchemy, chemistry, metallurgy

## Summary

The vast corpus attributed to Jabir ibn Hayyan — foundational Islamic alchemy on transmutation, metals, and elixirs.

## Why it matters

The root of both Islamic and European (Latin 'Geber') alchemy and early chemistry.
