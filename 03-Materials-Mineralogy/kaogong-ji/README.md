# Kaogong Ji

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 3rd c. BCE (Zhou)
**Origin / Tradition:** China
**Original language:** Chinese (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Craft, engineering standards

## Summary

The 'Record of Trades' — a chapter of the Zhouli detailing workshops, bronze-casting, chariot-making, and measurements.

## Why it matters

China's earliest technical/engineering handbook; defines official craft standards.
