# Tiangong Kaiwu

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** 1637 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** China (Song Yingxing, Ming)
**Original language:** Chinese (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Technology, materials, industry

## Summary

'The Exploitation of the Works of Nature' — a Ming encyclopaedia of Chinese technology: mining, metallurgy, salt, ceramics, and more.

## Why it matters

The most important pre-modern Chinese technological treatise; parallels Agricola's De Re Metallica.
