**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 9th–10th c. CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Persia/Iraq (al-Razi)
**Original language:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Alchemy, chemistry

## Summary

Al-Razi's 'Secret of Secrets' (Sirr al-Asrar) — a major Arabic alchemical work on apparatus, substances, and transmutation.

## Why it matters

One of the most influential medieval alchemical texts; shaped Latin alchemy via translation.
