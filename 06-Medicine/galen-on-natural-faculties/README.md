# On the Natural Faculties

**Author / Tradition:** Galen of Pergamon

**Era / Date:** c. 129–216 CE

**Origin / Tradition:** Roman Greece

**Language:** English (Brock trans., 1916, PD)

**Related manuscripts in this library:** Hippocrates; Avicenna Canon (pointer)

## Summary

Galen's physiological treatise on the three faculties (attractive, retentive, digestive) and humoral physiology.

## Why it matters

Systematised Greek anatomy/physiology that dominated medicine for ~1400 years.
