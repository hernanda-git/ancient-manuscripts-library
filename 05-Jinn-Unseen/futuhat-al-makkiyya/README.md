# Futūḥāt al-Makkiyya

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** c. 1238 CE (completed)
**Origin / Tradition:** Andalusia/North Africa (Ibn Arabi)
**Language of text:** Arabic (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Sufism, metaphysics, the unseen

## Summary

Ibn Arabi's 'Meccan Illuminations' — a vast encyclopedia of Sufi metaphysics, the cosmos, the unseen realms, and the imagination.

## Why it matters

The summit of Islamic mystical philosophy; defines the metaphysics of the unseen that frames jinn lore.
