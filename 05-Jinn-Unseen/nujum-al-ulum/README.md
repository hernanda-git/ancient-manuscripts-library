# Nujūm al-ʿUlūm — "Stars of the Sciences"

**Author:** various
**Era / Date:** 1571 CE
**Origin / Tradition:** Bijapur, Deccan (Muhammad Quli)
**Original language:** Persian (original)
**Modern discipline(s):** Occult sciences, cosmology, the unseen

## Summary

'Stars of the Sciences' — an illustrated encyclopaedia of the occult and cosmological sciences: jinn, peris, planets and their angels, talismans, geomancy, oneiromancy.

## Why it matters

A key witness to the Islamicate 'science of the unseen' (see its folder README for the authoritative manuscript reference).
